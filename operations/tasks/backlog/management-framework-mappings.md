# Map APSS to organizational management frameworks

## Idea

Research how APSS relates to established company and organizational management
frameworks, then compile the comparisons into a navigable wiki-style knowledge
artifact.

## Motivation

Clear mappings could help organizational users connect APSS concepts to
frameworks they already use, distinguish complementary practices from genuine
overlap or conflict, and avoid presenting renamed prior art as novel.

## Evidence

- APSS is domain-independent and explicitly does not prescribe a particular
  management method.
- The existing foundations-research item covers systems and adaptive-learning
  theory but does not yet define a focused organizational-management mapping or
  wiki deliverable.
- No management-framework comparison has been compiled yet.

## Open questions

- Which management frameworks and organizational contexts should be in scope?
- What comparison dimensions would make mappings consistent and useful?
- Should the artifact describe conceptual similarity, operational
  compatibility, evidence of effectiveness, or all three without conflating
  them?
- Where should the wiki live, and how will claims retain citations and
  provenance?
- How should this work coordinate with `apss.research-foundations`?
