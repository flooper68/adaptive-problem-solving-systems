# Fit and load performance of a conforming bracket have not been demonstrated

## Goal

Confirm the bracket fits the target assembly and survives its specified load.

## Evidence

No assembly fit or load-trial result exists for a conforming sample in this
illustrative task.

## Desired change

Assembly installs the bracket without rework and the part carries the specified
operating load.

## Signal

A sampled conforming part passes the assembly fit and specified load trial,
with rework and failures recorded.

## Strategy

After P1 accepts a conforming sample, install it in the target assembly and run
the specified load trial. Use fit, rework, deformation, failure, and field
evidence to decide whether the bracket solves the consumer problem.
