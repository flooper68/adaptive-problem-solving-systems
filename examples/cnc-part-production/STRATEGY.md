# CNC production strategy

## Goal

Produce the current bracket revision within drawing tolerances and confirm that
it fits the target assembly and survives its specified load.

## Approach

Use production and consumer evidence to maintain goal-relevant problems and
their strategies. Select batches or investigations that implement those
strategies, apply CNC machining and dimensional inspection, run assembly and
load trials, and update tooling or process parameters only from validated
learning.

## Guidance for problem grooming

- Keep drawing conformance and assembly outcome as separate problems because
  they require different evidence and may fail independently.
- Prioritize safety, required conformance, and consumer fit/load outcomes over
  optional production optimization.
- Prefer bounded trials before broad process changes when evidence is uncertain.
- Require manufacturing-lead approval for problem strategy, work selection,
  accepted risk, closure, and adaptation.
